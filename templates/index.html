Video to Text Summarization

About Project

We are generating textual summarization of the videographic content. Here we are working with educational videos only where the speaker is speaking something. From his/her speech, we are extracting the sentences by using the NLP model followed by summarization of the sentences by using Deep Learning models. For that you need to pass a youtube link of the video in the input box below. We will generate a summary of the video in the form of text.
